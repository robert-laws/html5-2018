Content Hierarchy

The Heading Level tags should aim to organize the most important headings in h1 tags and moving towards less important headings in h6 tags.

Search engines will treat the content of the h1 - h6 tags as keywords with different levels of importance.

Heading Level 2

In HTML5, the horizontal rule is used as a semantic document sectional division and not just for visual demarcation between page content.
